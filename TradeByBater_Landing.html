Frequently Asked Questions
Everything You Need to Know
Is TradeByBater really free?+
Yes — posting listings and making trades is completely free. Revenue comes from optional premium features like listing boosts and verified badges, not by taxing your trades.
What if someone tries to scam me?+
All users are phone-verified. For high-value trades, use our escrow service — your item is held safely until both parties confirm delivery. Our dispute mediation team handles any conflicts.
Do I need a bank account?+
No. That's the point. Barter does not require money, a bank account, or any financial infrastructure. Just a phone number and something to trade.
How does the AI assistant work?+
Our AI is available 24/7 on the website and both mobile apps. Ask it anything about listings, trades, safety, or platform features. It answers instantly and escalates to a human agent when needed.
Which Nigerian states are supported?+
All 36 states plus the FCT (Abuja). From Lagos Island to Maiduguri — every Nigerian is covered from Day 1.
Can businesses use TradeByBater?+
Absolutely. SMEs, artisans, freelancers, farms, and brands are all welcome. Dedicated Business Accounts with enhanced features are coming in Phase 2.
Can I trade with someone outside Nigeria?+
Cross-border trade launches in Phase 2 with West Africa, expands through Phase 3 across the continent, and reaches global scale in Phase 4 by 2027.
Is my personal data safe?+
Yes. We comply with NDPR (Nigeria Data Protection Regulation) and use industry-standard encryption for all personal data. We never sell your information to third parties.
