## Smart Intent Recognition System

The new system has 13 main intents:

1. **Greetings** - `hi, hello, hey, whatsup, howdy, sup, yo`
   - Returns warm, personalized responses

2. **Name** - `your name, who are you, call you, who's this`
   - Introduces itself properly as AI Assistant

3. **How to List** - `list something, how to list, post item, create listing, upload`
   - Full step-by-step guidance

4. **How to Trade** - `how to trade, find item, request trade, make offer, browse listings`
   - Trading workflow explained

5. **Locations** - `where, location, state, city, available in, Lagos, Abuja, Kano`
   - Covers all 36 Nigerian states

6. **Cost/Pricing** - `cost, price, free, charge, fee, pay, money`
   - Explains the free model

7. **Safety/Security** - `safe, security, scam, protect, trusted, verified, fraudulent`
   - Comprehensive security features

8. **Payment Methods** - `payment, bank, transfer, money, cash, how do i pay`
   - Explains direct barter approach

9. **Categories** - `category, categories, what can i trade, what items, what's available`
   - Lists all 8 trading categories

10. **Trust Ratings** - `rating, reputation, trust score, reviews, how do i build`
    - How to build credibility

11. **Dispute Resolution** - `dispute, problem, conflict, disagree, issue, complaint`
    - Quick support process

12. **Mobile Apps** - `app, mobile, android, iphone, ios, download, phone`
    - App status and web alternatives

13. **Getting Started** - `getting started, how do i start, new user, beginner`
    - Onboarding journey

14. **Culture/Nigerian Focus** - `nigerian, african, culture, tradition, barter tradition`
    - Celebrates African barter heritage
